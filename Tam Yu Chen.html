What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks are typically aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting normal business processes.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails trying to steal your data.
  • Malware: Malicious software such as viruses and ransomware.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating people into revealing confidential information.
  • Man-in-the-Middle Attacks: Intercepting data between two parties.

Top Cyber Security Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Keep your software and systems updated.
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails or links.
  • Regularly back up your important data.
