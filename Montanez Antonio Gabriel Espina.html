5 types of Cyber-Attacks and How They Work

Cyber-attacks are becoming more common as the digital world grows. It's important to know how these attacks work to protect ourselves online.

Here are some common types of cyber attacks you could possibly encounter.

1. Malware

Malicious software that can damage your data and steal personal data.

For example, downloading a game from a shady website can make you accidentally download a virus

2. Ransomware

A type of malware that locks your files and demands payment to unlock them.

One example includes, opening a suspicious email which then displays you need to pay a certain amount of money to access.

3.Phishing

Fake emails, messages or even websites that trick you into giving away personal information.

For example, you recieve an email thinking its from your bank asking to verify your account. However, the link brings you to a fake website pretending to be your bank to get your personal information.

4.MITM (Man in the Middle)

A hacker secretly intercepts messages between two people or systems, often on public Wi-Fi.

For example, You connect to free public wifi. A hacker intercepts your connection and steals your login info.

5.DOS (Denial of Service)
